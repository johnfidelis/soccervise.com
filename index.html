Understanding Soccer Betting Odds

 

Soccervise makes use of the European decimal odds format, which always reflects the ratio of your total payout to your stake. For instance, if you see a match odds of 2.20 for a particular game, then a $10 bet would return a total payout of $22 (this actually means that your $10 stake makes a $12 profit) if eventually your prediction plays out.

 

Common Types of Soccer Bets

 

One of the most popular betting options in football are the 1X2 bets:

  • 1: The home team wins the match.
  • X: The match ends in a draw.
  • 2: The away team wins the match.

 

At the same time, you can as well look into double chance bets:

  • 1X: The home team wins or draws.
  • X2: Away team wins or draws.
  • 12: Away team or Home team win.

 

Example: if you see a bet that is tagged as 1 @ 2.30, it basically means that it is a bet on the Home team with a total odds of 2.30. That actually means that a $10 bet would give you $23 in return while taking off $13 profit if the Home team wins the match.
